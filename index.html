About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio in progress. My name is Brandon and I enjoy long walks on the beach, going to bingo, and writing biographies.

Just kidding, I don't like any of those things! I've been making basic websites since adolescence - bringing HTML GOODIES and some PHP 4 book I've long forgotten almost everywhere I went. Visiting my grandparents was a treat as I'd get to hop on their Windows NT 3.1 machine and go to town.

The highest level of education I've completed up to now is a Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy from Virginia Commonwealth University. I've freelanced as a front-end web developer for the past couple of years. Making basic websites out of html, css, and some javascript.

I'm currently trying to get out of the 'cave' and learn modern javascript frameworks, libraries, and everything that goes with it to become a more capable developer. My means of achieving this goal are through attending The George Washington University Full-Stack Coding Boot Camp, reading as many technical books as I can get my hands on, and completing online courses and tutorials.

In my time off I like to ride my bike, go camping, and hang out with my dog Buffy - but I usually end up having to fix my old Saab... again.
